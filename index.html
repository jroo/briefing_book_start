About

Briefing Book: New START Treaty is the first in a series of applications from the nonpartisan Sunlight Foundation that aim to provide citizens with access to the research and opinions that influence the legislative process. From links to editorials, all the way down to the text of the legislation itself, we aim to empower citizens with the same information that legislators use to make their decisions.

The purpose of this application is to provide a nonpartisan look at the resources used in the legislative process. Sunlight Foundation is not advocating for any particular outcome related to this issue. If you feel that an important resource is missing from this collection, please let us know.
